7th****
Room

An hour by car from Luleä (Kallax) airport

Inside the room you will find panoramic windows facing the river valley and the northern lights - the bedrooms have skylight windows for you to view the bright summer night sky. At the centre of the room there is a terrace, formed by a net floor with a naturally growing pine in the middle.

Through the net floor you can see 10 meters below you and behind a transparent railing lies in the vast expanse of the Lule River Valley. A breath taking, awe-inspiring and perfectly safe place to be. Also, it is practically mosquito-free, since mosquitoes don't usually fly this high.
